Research

Particle-flow reconstruction for MAIA

Adapting the PandoraPFA particle-flow chain to the MAIA muon-collider detector geometry, and measuring how well it separates and calibrates the particles coming out of a collision.

Shower profiles & photon identification

Tracing how PandoraPFA's longitudinal pseudo-layer scheme treats the in-calorimeter solenoid — and why ignoring those few radiation lengths of upstream material biases photon ID. Built a monitoring algorithm to pull shower-profile variables into ROOT.

Detector simulation & software

Day-to-day work on the MuColl stack — DD4hep, iLCSoft, k4geo — running inside containerized, Spack-managed environments on a computing cluster, with the analysis side in Julia.
